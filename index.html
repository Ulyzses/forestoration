Science Behind the issue

Deforestation is defined as the wide cutting or clearcutting of a large area of trees. This causes large consequences to our environment as well as people living near forests as trees help lessen flash floods and excess carbon emissions in our atmosphere.

Philippine Status

Every year, the forest management bureau (FMB) under the department of environment and natural resources (DENR) releases the philippine forestry statistics which helps filipinos become more aware of the current status of our forests.

Learn more

SDGs Involved

The 2030 agenda for sustainable development is a blueprint adopted by all 2015 united nation member states which aims to improve peace and prosperity for the people and the planet. to further guide us towards this goal, the 17 sustainable development goals (SDGS) were made, each having its own specific goal and indicators.

How to Help

As students, we can contribute to the prevention of deforestation by volunteering or giving donations to various organizations with the goal of protecting our forests. There exists multple organizations, both local and international, with different, yet effective activities on doing so.
