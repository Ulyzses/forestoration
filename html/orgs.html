How to Help

Our abilities as the youth may seem limited at first, but don't underestimate our potential. Small actions can make great changes!

1. Participate in TREE PLANTING programs

Start from scratch. Promote regrowth.

2. Make some CHANGES to your lifestyle

Reduce paper consumption, make use of reusable bags, or recycle when possible.

3. Raise Social Awareness

Inform and educate. Use the power of technology and social media and spread the word!

4. Propose a law requiring grades 10 and 12 students to plant at least 10 trees before graduation

5. Join Environmental Organizations

There are tons out there with various advocacies. Research more and join forces!
